How to reduce consumption per indicator
Electricity
  • Install motion sensors in classrooms to auto-cut lights.
  • Power off all equipment outside school hours.
  • Replace fluorescent fixtures with LED lighting.
  • Apply green coding principles: optimise software to reduce CPU load and server energy usage.
Potential saving: ~20–30%
Water
  • Fit aerators on all taps to reduce flow without loss of pressure.
  • Collect rainwater for irrigation of green areas.
  • Implement a periodic leak-detection programme.
  • Install dual-flush mechanisms in all toilets.
Potential saving: ~15–25%
Office supplies
  • "Paper-zero" policy: digitise all submissions and handouts.
  • Set double-sided printing as the default on all printers.
  • Reuse single-sided draft paper for internal documents.
  • Centralise purchasing to avoid unnecessary over-stocking.
Potential saving: ~30–40%
Cleaning products
  • Switch to certified eco-label products (EU Ecolabel / Nordic Swan).
  • Use automatic dosing dispensers to prevent waste.
  • Purchase concentrated products to reduce packaging waste.
  • Schedule cleaning outside peak-activity hours for efficiency.
Potential saving: ~10–20%
